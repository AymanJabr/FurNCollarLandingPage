Why Choose Us?

Our services are comprehensive, for all your animal-care needs.
Our staff has extensive experience in handling animals, to realize their maximum comfort and well being.
Our customers are mostly repeat customers who have gotten used to entrusting us with their pets.

Although we always have staff in our facilities, customers can only drop off and claim their pets at specific hours, this is to make sure that the pets in our care are not disturbed by overnight visitors.
We are open Monday-Friday from 8am to 8pm, Saturday-Sunday from 10am to 12pm.
We tailor our prices on a customer by customer basis, this is to ensure that each customer gets the best service for his pets while ensuring that pricing stays flexible.
We have multiple payment plans, to make it easier for our customers to pay as they best see fit.
Although this depends largely on the specific pet and his needs, grooming services take around 1 or 2 days, veterinarian services are usually done by appointment, but we might have to keep your pet with us overnight for certain cases, dog training services can vary from a few days to a few weeks, holiday pet care services are usually set by the customer.
